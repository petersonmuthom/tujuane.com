Features

Real-Time Chat lets you connect instantly with the people you match with on tujuane. Messages are fast, private, and seamless, making conversations feel natural and effortless. You can get to know someone in a comfortable, secure space designed for students. It’s the easiest way to keep the spark alive and build genuine campus connections.

The Activity Feed keeps your experience fresh and exciting by showing profiles recommended just for you. Discover new students, see who’s active, and explore suggested matches based on your interests. It helps you connect faster by highlighting people you’re most likely to vibe with. Everything updates in real time, keeping your campus connections lively and engaging.

Profile Creation lets students express who they are in a simple, authentic way. Add your bio, interests, course, and photos to showcase your personality. Your profile becomes your space to stand out and connect with people who vibe with you. It’s quick, easy, and designed to help others understand your story at a glance.
