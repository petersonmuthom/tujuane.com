Safety First

Your safety comes first on tujuane. Every student is verified, so you can meet real people around campus without worrying about fake profiles or spam. We make sure your experience is genuine and trustworthy.

Privacy is always protected. Your personal details stay secure, and you control what others see. Chats are private and encrypted, giving you confidence to connect freely. You can also block or report anyone who makes you uncomfortable.

We guide you to stay safe while exploring connections. Follow simple tips: meet in public places, avoid sharing sensitive info, and trust your instincts at all times. tujuane lets you connect, chat, and build relationships securely and confidently.
